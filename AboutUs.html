About Hanzon & Sjöberg

Hanzon & Sjöberg was founded in 1997 by the two friends Eva Hanzon and Elisabet Sjöberg. They have both worked with interior decoration for a long time and have extensive experience in decorating both in homes but also public environments. 
Their expertise and their classical style in interior design, color and shape spread initially within the circle of acquaintances, which have since attracted attention throughout Stockholm. 
The two friends have been living in Marbella for several years back. They have also been attracted to their solid interior style and expertise that helped many Scandinavians to settle in their Spanish homes. .

How we work

Hanzon & Sjöberg will assist you with interior design, drawings and project management of your place. We will make suggestions for single rooms or your entire home or workplace. Our work includes product and material suggestions, drawings, orders and delivery as well as project management of the work itself.
We will be happy to have a meeting at your place when you tell us about your wishes. Together we discuss a plan on how exactly your project could be implemented. After the meeting, we will present a proposal that accurately specifies what is part of our mission. We work within any budget, big or small. You can start from scratch or work with us using your existing furniture pieces.
Our consultant price is €75/hour
