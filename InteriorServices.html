Interior help

The service Interior from start to finish begins with a meeting in where we together talk about expectations, needs, style, etc. Then we create a complete design proposal containing color scheme, product collage with associated shopping list and a simpler plan drawing with the accompanying description. If necessary, we also help with the purchase of furniture, etc. according to the meeting proposal, coordinate deliveries and furnishings on-site, in whole or in part.
